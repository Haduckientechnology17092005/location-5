HOI AN TRAVEL TOUR GUIDE

Hoi An Unique Street Food Tour (price : 700.000VND)

Picking up time :
3.30 pm to 6.00pm (afternoon tour)
One of the best ways to get to know a location is by tasting the local food. Join us and explore culinary delights in Hoi An in 4hours with our local English speaking guide

Details:

Pickup

Be picked up at the Meeting Point (Godiva - Dinks & Cakes at 11 Hoang Dieu Street, Hoi An ) by our friendly local guides.
After that, you will walk through the city streets. There are many delicious stops that you can enjoy the most authentic Hoi An specialties from our favorite vendors, who have been feeding locals for generations. THESE ARE MUST TRY HOI AN LOCAL FOODS!

Action

This is also MORE THAN A FOOD TOUR because you can learn some fascinating history and culture about Hoi An from our guides
End of the tour! So eat as the locals eat, and see what the locals see!
